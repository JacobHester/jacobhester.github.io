Weather Website

Audience:

This website is designed for users who want the most important aspects of the weather displayed in a quick and orderly fashion. This website will be seen and used by people from the age of 12 and older. It will be accessed primarily by mobile phones and will be easy to load and navigate.

Persona: Shooter Magavin

Age: 40
Gender: Male
Highest Level of Education: Bachelors
Professional Background: Works in a professional setting. Golfs recreationally

This individual will visit the site to check the weather before he leaves his house to go to work. He will also use this site to plan his golf trips and days out. This site will serve as a everyday local weather check aswell as an accurate planning tool.

The user accesses his laptop computer and mobile smart phone on a regular daily basis. This site will be visited primarily on a smart phone. They have all the current software that is available. This user will spend more time on their mobile phone or tablet then they do on their computer.

The user is motivated to choose this website because of the accuracy and usability level at which this site operates. He doesn't want anything other than the weather.

"I want to know the weather today and be able to see the weather for this weekend. I have golfing to do."
